TARSIER 117

November 15, 2025

TaRSIER 117 Operation Center (also known as Province of Bohol Provincial Disaster Risk Reduction Management Operation Center Tarsier 117) is an integrated emergency response unit for the whole of Bohol that is located at the Tagbilaran City and offers 24/7 public safety support. It deals with emergency calls and manages the response to medical emergencies, fires, accidents, rescues, and disaster incidents throughout the province. TaRSIER 117 also provides life-saving skills training and emergency preparedness workshops for community residents and collaborates with other response agencies to enhance disaster preparedness. The Provincial Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Office (PDRRMO) supervises its operations.
