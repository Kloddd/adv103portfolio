DYNATA PHILIPPINES INC

November 13, 2025

Dynata Philippines (Cebu) is the Philippine division of a world-wide market research and data company that obtains and provides first-party data from surveyed consumers and professionals to facilitate businesses in their decision-making process. An office is located in Lapu-Lapu City, Cebu where the staff is engaged in carrying out surveys, data collection, customer/market research support, and providing related services. Marketing, advertising, consulting, and research firms all over the world use Dynata's data and insights to get better acquainted with the audience and to fine-tune their strategies.
