MATA TECHNOLOGIES

November 14, 2025

Mata Technologies is a tech company based in Cebu, which mainly develops virtual reality maps and virtual tours in 360° for tourism destinations and real estate properties in the Philippines. It allows the public to visit and experience cities, attractions, and properties digitally and interactively. VR and 360° media are the tools Mata uses to make the whole process of tourism more engaging and to showcase the destinations and homes in a modern way.
