UP CEBU

November 12, 2025

CeBu InIT (UP Cebu Business Incubator for Information Technology) at the University of the Philippines Cebu is a tech business incubator. It provides support to startups at the early-stage and growth phases, focusing mainly on the IT and creative industries. CeBu InIT's offerings include mentorship, training, access to facilities, business development support, and connections to financiers and other networks. The ultimate aim is to assist inventors in transforming their concepts into infrared, viable, and commercial tech businesses.
