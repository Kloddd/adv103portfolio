RIVAN IT CEBU

November 13, 2025

Rivan IT Cebu is the Cebu branch of Rivan IT, which is a technology training and educational provider with a worldwide presence, offering technology courses and certifications like CCNA, Linux, cybersecurity, and others in the field of networking and IT. It mainly conducts hands-on training in order to impart to students and professionals the practical IT competencies required for different careers in IT. The Cebu facility is both a computer training school and an educational consulting firm where learners can sign up for boot camps and technical courses to gain the necessary skills for landing IT jobs.
